[[def: controller, constrollers]]

~ A controlling entity that can cryptographically prove the control authority (signing and rotation) over an [[ref: AID]] as well as make changes on the associated [[ref: KEL]]. A controller may consist of multiple controlling entities in a [[ref: multi-signature]] scheme. 

~ More detail: [WebOfTrust glossary](https://weboftrust.github.io/WOT-terms/docs/glossary/controller).
