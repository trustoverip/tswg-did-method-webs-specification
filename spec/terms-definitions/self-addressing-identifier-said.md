[[def: self-addressing-identifier, self-addressing identifier, SAID, SAIDs]]

~ An identifier that is uniquely and cryptographically bound to a serialization of data (content-addressable) while also being included as a component in that serialization (self-referential), as defined by the [[ref:  CESR specification]]. 

~ More detail: [WebOfTrust glossary](https://weboftrust.github.io/WOT-terms/docs/glossary/self-addressing-identifier).
