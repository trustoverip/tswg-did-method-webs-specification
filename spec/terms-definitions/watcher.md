[[def: watcher, watchers]]

~ An entity that keeps a copy of a [[ref: KERL]] of an [[ref: AID]] to detect duplicity of [[ref: key events]], as defined by the [KERI whitepaper](https://github.com/SmithSamuelM/Papers/blob/master/whitepapers/KERI_WP_2.x.web.pdf). 

~ More detail: [WebOfTrust glossary](https://weboftrust.github.io/WOT-terms/docs/glossary/watcher).
