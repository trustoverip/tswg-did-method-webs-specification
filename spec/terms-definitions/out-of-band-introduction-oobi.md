[[def: out-of-band-introduction, out-of-band introduction, OOBI, OOBIs, OOBI specification]]

~ A protocol for discovering verifiable information on an [[ref: AID]] or a [[ref: SAID]], as defined by the [[ref:  KERI specification]]. The OOBI by itself is insecure, and the information discovered by the OOBI must be verified. 

~ More detail: [WebOfTrust glossary](https://weboftrust.github.io/WOT-terms/docs/glossary/out-of-band-introduction).
