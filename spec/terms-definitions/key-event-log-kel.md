[[def: key-event-log, key event log, KEL, KELs]]

~ A verifiable append-only log of [[ref: key events]] for an [[ref: AID]] that is both backward and forward-chained, as defined by the [[ref: KERI specification]]. 

~ More detail: [WebOfTrust glossary](https://weboftrust.github.io/WOT-terms/docs/glossary/key-event-log).
