[[def: transaction-event-log, transaction event log, TEL, TELs]]

~ A verifiable append-only log of transaction data that are cryptographically anchored to a [[ref: KEL]]. The transaction events of a TEL may be used to establish the issuance or revocation state of [[ref: ACDCs]].
